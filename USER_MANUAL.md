# PetCare Services: Complete Training & User Guide

Welcome to PetCare Services! This guide provides step-by-step instructions for both Pet Owners and Service Providers to navigate the mobile and web applications effectively.

---

## 1. Getting Started
### Account Creation
1. **Open the App**: Launch PetCare on your mobile device or visit the website.
2. **Select Role**: Choose **"I am a Pet Owner"** or **"I am a Service Provider"**.
3. **Register**: Click "Sign Up" and provide your email, full name, and phone number.
4. **Login**: Use your credentials to access your personalized dashboard.

---

## 2. Guide for Pet Owners
### How to Set Up Your Pet's Profile
1. Go to **My Profile** (User icon).
2. Click **"+ Add New Pet"**.
3. Enter the pet's **Name**, select **Type** (Dog, Cat, etc.), and **Breed**.
4. Add **Medical Notes** or special requirements (e.g., "Allergic to peanuts").
5. Save the profile. You can now select this pet during bookings.

### How to Book a Service
1. **Search**: Tap the search bar or "Find a Provider" button.
2. **Filter**: Enter your **City** and select the service you need (e.g., Grooming).
3. **Select Provider**: Browse results, checking **Ratings** and **Reviews**. Tap a card to see their full profile.
4. **Pick a Service**: Select the specific service (e.g., "Full Spa Groom").
5. **Schedule**: 
   - Tap a highlighted date on the **Calendar**.
   - Select an available **Time Slot**.
6. **Book**: Add any specific notes for the provider and tap **"Confirm Booking"**.

### Managing Appointments & Payments
- **View Bookings**: Access the "My Bookings" tab to see Pending, Confirmed, or Past sessions.
- **Cancel/Reschedule**: Tap an appointment to find options to change or cancel (available for Pending/Confirmed status).
- **Pay**: Once a provider confirms, tap **"Pay Now"** in the appointment details to settle the invoice via Stripe.

---

## 3. Guide for Service Providers
### Setting Up Your Business
1. **Complete Profile**: Go to your dashboard and select **"My Profile"**.
2. **Business Details**: Enter your Company Name, Address, and a compelling Description.
3. **Set Pricing**: Add the services you offer and your **Hourly Rate**.
4. **Stripe Connection**: Click **"Connect with Stripe"** to enable automated payouts to your bank account.

### Managing Your Availability
1. Tap **"Manage Availability"**.
2. Select a date on the calendar.
3. Tap **"Add Slot"** and set the start and end times.
4. **Tip**: Keep your calendar updated to avoid booking conflicts!

### Handling Incoming Requests
1. **Dashboard**: New requests appear in **"Today's Schedule"** or via the **Bell Icon** (Notifications).
2. **Action**: Tap the request to view details (Pet type, notes, time).
3. **Decision**: Select **Confirm** to accept or **Decline** if you are unavailable.

---

## 4. Understanding Statuses & Icons
- 🟡 **Pending**: Waiting for the provider to accept.
- 🟢 **Confirmed**: Booking is locked in.
- 🔵 **Completed**: Service is finished; owner can now leave a review.
- 🔴 **Cancelled**: Booking is voided.
- ⭐ **Star Icon**: Represents the provider's average quality rating from previous customers.
- 🐾 **Paw Icon**: Represents Pet information or specific pet care tips.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting & Tips
- **Errors on Logout**: If you see errors after logging out, simply restart the app to clear the session cache.
- **Missing Providers**: Try increasing your search radius or checking nearby cities.
- **Payments**: Ensure your internet connection is stable when clicking "Pay Now" to avoid transaction failures.
